I'm an aspiring product designer based in Tallinn, Estonia.

I enjoy turning a complex idea into a beautiful yet simple design, where every element I place has a meaning and purpose.

Although my experience might be scarce, I'm a fast learner and can hold my own at every challenge thrown my way. I started learning product design in 2019 thanks to Codesters club, a Digital Product program started by the Riesenkampff Foundation. At the end of 2019 Codesters Club held a contest among all its 51 students and the top 3 got an internship in Germany, but due to the Coronavirus, this internship has to wait. Fortunately, I had a great opportunity to be an intern as a product designer at Founderly for 4 months learning the basics of how to work in a company.

When working on a new project I start with simple questions - Who's it for? What is the problem we're trying to solve? Is THIS the best solution? Asking a lot of questions is crucial for me since I have to fully understand the project I'm working on. When I have a good understanding of what I have to do I get to work making an early prototype and then going from there.
